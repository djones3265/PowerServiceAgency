About Us

Power Service Agency began as "John S. Baron and Associates" in 1948 in Music City - Nashville, TN. Today PSA is stronger than ever in its third generation of the Baron family. With a legacy spanning nearly 75 years, Power Service Agency is one of the most experienced agencies in the country.


John's first line was Preformed Line Products, which PSA still proudly represents today. In addition to PLP, PSA represents Hughes Brothers Inc., Prysmian Group (formerly Pirelli Cables), and several other top-line suppliers of the Energy & Communications Market Sectors.


Our outside Sales Force with extensive experience covers our Territory of Tennessee and Kentucky and is always available to help our customers in any way possible.
